Privacy Policy

1. Who We Are

TipperLink Ltd is a UK-registered company operating the TipperLink digital exchange platform at tipperlink.co.uk. We are the data controller for the personal information we collect through this website and platform. If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at hello@tipperlink.com.

2. What Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you register your interest, create an account, or use the platform:

  • Name and company name
  • Email address and telephone number
  • Vehicle type, fleet size and operating areas
  • Business type and load volume (for suppliers)
  • Load postings, quotes submitted, and job completion data
  • Payment information (processed securely via Stripe — we do not store card details)

We also collect technical data automatically when you use the site, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time on site (via Google Analytics).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the TipperLink exchange platform
  • To match load postings with relevant hauliers and notify you of matching loads
  • To process subscription payments and send invoices
  • To build and display your public haulier rating and profile
  • To send platform updates, new features, and service communications
  • To improve the platform through aggregated analytics
  • To comply with our legal obligations

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data under the following legal bases: contract performance (to deliver the services you've subscribed to), legitimate interests (to operate and improve our platform), and consent (for marketing communications, which you can withdraw at any time).

5. Who We Share Data With

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We share data only with:

  • Stripe — for payment processing
  • Supabase — for secure database hosting
  • Google Analytics — for anonymised usage analytics
  • Formspree — for registration form submissions
  • Other suppliers and hauliers on the platform, but only the data necessary to facilitate a specific load transaction

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you close your account, we will delete your personal data within 90 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or financial compliance purposes (typically 6 years for financial records).

7. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the ICO (ico.org.uk)

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@tipperlink.com.

8. Cookies

We use cookies for analytics (Google Analytics) and to maintain your session when logged in. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling analytics cookies does not affect your ability to use the platform.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify registered members of any material changes by email. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
