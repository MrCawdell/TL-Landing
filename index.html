How It Works

Post it. Quote it. Done.

From load posted to haulier confirmed in minutes — not phone calls, not spreadsheets, not WhatsApp chains.

01
📋

Post Your Load

Choose your vehicle type — artic, 8-wheel, 4-wheel, grab or walking floor. Tonnage calculates automatically. Live on the exchange in seconds.

02
💬

Hauliers Quote

Verified UK hauliers see loads matching their vehicle type and submit competitive quotes — with their completion rate and rating attached to every one.

03
✅

Check Ratings & Accept

Review quotes alongside each haulier's verified score. Accept the best one — confirmed instantly, others automatically declined. Job done.

Platform Features

Built for UK tipper haulage and bulk material supply. No bloat, no complexity.

🚛

Vehicle Type Selection

Choose from artic tipper (30t), 8-wheel tipper (20t), 4-wheel tipper (10t), 8-wheel grab lorry (16t) or walking floor artic (30t). Loads and tonnage calculate automatically.

⭐

Verified Haulier Ratings

Every haulier has a public reliability score built from real job history. Completion rates and reviews visible before accepting any quote.

🏭

Aggregate Marketplace

Coming soon — post bulk material requirements or supply capacity. Quarries and sand and gravel suppliers quote directly. Pair with a haulier in one platform.

⚡

Instant Load Posting

Post a full load in under 2 minutes. Material, route, vehicle type, rate and date. Live on the exchange immediately with hauliers notified.

⚠️

No-Show Protection

Hauliers who accept and don't complete receive an automatic strike. Three strikes triggers platform review. Your operations are protected.

📱

Works on Any Device

Fully responsive — use TipperLink from your phone on-site, a tablet in the cab, or at your desk. No app download required.
